University of Engineering and Technology Mardan

The University of Engineering & Technology, Mardan is a public university located in Mardan, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan.

Visit Us To Know More

Courses We Offer

With 18 undergraduate and 24 graduate level degrees to choose from, UETM has a programme for everyone.
Our five Schools have nurtured leaders in areas ranging from entrepreneurship, economics, mathematics, engineering , education, writing, politics and business.

Undergraduate Programs:

UETM offers diverse undergraduate
programs providing a solid foundation in engineering and technology. These Bachelor of Science (BS) degrees emphasize fundamental concepts, practical skills, and real-world applications. With dedicated faculty and modern laboratories, students engage in hands-on learning, preparing for successful careers in their fields upon
graduation.

Graduate Programs:

UETM provides extensive graduate programs to deepen expertise in engineering and technology. These Master of Science (MS) degrees focus on advanced theoretical knowledge, innovative research, and specialized skills. With experienced faculty and cutting-edge facilities, students undertake rigorous academic work and projects, readying for leadership roles and advanced opportunities.

Post Graduation Programs:

UETM offers diverse postgraduate programs fostering advanced scholarship in engineering and technology. These Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degrees emphasize in-depth research, critical analysis, and new knowledge creation. Supported by distinguished faculty and premier research centers, students engage in significant research, preparing for academic careers, high-level industry roles, and technological innovation.

Our Facilities

UETM boasts state-of-the-art facilities, ensuring a conducive environment for academic excellence and personal growth.
